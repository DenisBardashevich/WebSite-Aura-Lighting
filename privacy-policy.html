Compliance and Data Protection

At Aura Lighting, we take your privacy seriously. We comply with data protection laws such as the GDPR and CCPA, ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly.

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access and breaches. Your trust is important to us, and we continuously update our privacy practices to align with the latest legal and security standards.

Data Collection and Usage

Aura Lighting collects personal data to improve your shopping experience. This may include contact details, order history, and website interactions.

We use this information to process orders, enhance our services, and provide personalized recommendations. Your data is never shared with third parties without your consent, and we take all necessary precautions to keep it safe.

Transparency and Accountability

We believe in complete transparency when it comes to how we handle your personal information. Our privacy policy outlines our practices clearly, ensuring you know exactly how your data is collected and used.

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, you can contact us, and we will be happy to assist you.

User Rights and Choices

As a valued customer, you have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information. You can also request to opt out of data collection for marketing purposes.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@AuraLighting.com. We are committed to ensuring that your data is handled with care and respect.
